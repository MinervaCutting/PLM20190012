Ilunion Atrium

Hotel ilunion Atrium is located at Ciudad Lineal quarter located in the outskirts of Madrid, but not too far from the city centre. Only 10 minutes by coach.

This hotel stands out thanks to its contemporary design; the robust cement-strucutred building, with high ceilings, combines urban materials with steel and glass panelling..

Ilunion Atrium features a wide range of services and facilities for the group; gym on the upper floor, wokring areas with connections in which to work quietly with your laptop, terrace - cafe with Madrid views, etc.
